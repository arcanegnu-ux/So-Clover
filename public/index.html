    Buttons and Shortcuts

    Grey rectangles with text inside them are buttons. Click on them :)

    To input your clues, click on the side of the clover you would like to type in,

    then type your clue. Press enter or click elsewhere to lock in your clue.

    When reconstructing other player's boards, all players can see the cards move.

    To move a card, simply click and drag it. To place it on the board, drag it to

    an empty slot, and it will snap into place. To rotate the cards clockwise, click

    on them once. To rotate the cards counterclockwise, close your eyes and click on

    them three times really fast.
